About Us



THE COME UP


Undrafted was founded on 2017 thru our love for basketball and hip-hop. What was a dream that started from a simple hobby became a product itself.



A humble beginning from The 1600, started thru meet-ups. Opened a pop-up shop every release. From 30 people buying to selling out within an hour. Who would've thought from making simple designs that resonates with other people will build a community.



The underdogs. This is for the few who never made it, and for those who are trying to get it. The motivated. The first one to enter and the last one to leave. The reason why you started.
